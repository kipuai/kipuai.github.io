## Voice & tone

- **Confident, not braggy.** State the value plainly; let credentials do the boasting.
- **Editorial.** Short declarative sentences. Almost no marketing adjectives ("cutting-edge", "world-class", "innovative"). One or two strong verbs per paragraph.
- **Bilingual-native.** Spanish reads as Peruvian-LatAm professional, not translated. Anglicisms are fine where the term is industry-standard ("AI-native", "MLOps", "roadmap", "stack"). Don't translate them awkwardly.
- **Restrained.** When in doubt, cut a sentence.
- **"AI-native"** stays untranslated in Spanish — Latin American tech audiences read it that way already.

**EN**

> Not because the technology isn't ready — but because the organization isn't. Data is fragmented. Talent is missing. Governance is unclear. Use cases are picked by enthusiasm, not leverage.
>
> The Kipu Readiness Index is the first step we take with every client. Three to four weeks. A clear score. A prioritized roadmap. No fluff.

**ES**
> ## La mayoría de las estrategias de IA fracasan antes de empezar.
> No porque la tecnología no esté lista — sino porque la organización no lo está. La data está fragmentada. Falta talento. La gobernanza no es clara. Los casos de uso se eligen por entusiasmo, no por palanca.
>
> El Kipu Readiness Index es el primer paso que damos con cada cliente. Tres a cuatro semanas. Un puntaje claro. Un roadmap priorizado. Sin relleno.

## 2.3 The five dimensions

> Visual: a five-point diagram (radar or sequential). Each dimension below gets ~2 sentences in the design.

**EN**
> ### 1. Strategic Clarity
> Are AI investments tied to specific business outcomes, or driven by pressure to "do something with AI"?
>
> ### 2. Data Foundation
> Is your data accessible, governed, and trustworthy enough to power decisions and models?
>
> ### 3. Talent & Operating Model
> Do you have the roles, structures, and decision rights to execute? Or is AI sitting in one team, isolated from the business?
>
> ### 4. Use Case Portfolio
> Which use cases will move the P&L? Which are noise? We score them by impact, feasibility, and time to value.
>
> ### 5. Governance & Risk
> Are policies, controls, and accountability in place to scale AI responsibly?

**ES**
> ### 1. Claridad Estratégica
> ¿Las inversiones en IA están atadas a resultados de negocio específicos, o nacen de la presión por "hacer algo con IA"?
>
> ### 2. Fundación de Datos
> ¿Tu data está accesible, gobernada y es lo suficientemente confiable para impulsar decisiones y modelos?
>
> ### 3. Talento y Modelo Operativo
> ¿Tienes los roles, estructuras y derechos de decisión para ejecutar? ¿O la IA vive aislada en un equipo, lejos del negocio?
>
> ### 4. Portafolio de Casos de Uso
> ¿Qué casos de uso mueven el P&L? ¿Cuáles son ruido? Los puntuamos por impacto, factibilidad y time-to-value.
>
> ### 5. Gobernanza y Riesgo
> ¿Existen políticas, controles y accountability para escalar IA de manera responsable?
